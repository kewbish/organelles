Prokaria City - Working Together to Take Over the World Thrive

Prokaryotes can be one of two things: bacteria, or archaebacteria (a distant relative of bacteria). All prokaryotes are unicellular organisms, relatively simpler than eukaryotic cells. However, prokaryotes, especially bacteria, love to work together. Some work with other prokaryotes, or with a host organism, which allows them to benefit, usually on resources.

Prokaria City is still small, and we plan on staying that way. However, our neighbouring cities, Archia and Bactia, cooperate to share resources with us.
